A Quasi-Direct-Drive Cable Actuation System for an Lightweight and Highly-Backdrivable Knee Exoskeleton PDF
Shuangyue Yu, Yanjun Li, Tzu-Hao Huang, Junlin Wang, Xiao Li, Hao Su
Abstract: Quasi-direct drive paradigm can effectively improve the torque density and torque control performance, which are two vital points for a safe and comfortable exoskeleton. Following this paradigm, this paper introduces a novel knee-exoskeleton design combining the advantages of a high torque density motor and a bi-directional cable transmission system. The exoskeleton is lightweight and highly backdrivable. Performance test also demonstrated that the torque control bandwidth is up to 28Hz. These characteristics allow a safe interaction between the user and the exoskeleton.
